About us


Founded in 2016, HealthyPaws is a local veterinary clinic located in Kanata, Ontario

At HealthyPaws, we make it our mission to provide quality medical care and services for your beloved cats and dogs.

Our team of certified doctors and specialists assure you that we are there for you and your pets whenever you need.

Services


Wellness Checkup

Wellness checkups evaluate your pets overall health, detect problems before they become serious, and keep them on track to live a long, healthy life.

Price: $50

Dentistry

Dental exams and cleanings are essential to protect your pet from many health problems and help them maintain a healthy and clean mouth.

Price: $75

Nutrition

Proper diet and nutrition can help your pet fight against disease, maintain proper weight, and promote their overall well-being and help them lead healthy lives.

Price: $65

Parasite Control

Parasites such as fleas and ticks can be very damaging to your pets health. Preventive measures should be taken year round to prevent potential outbreaks.

Price: $40

Diagnostic Services

We offer a large range of diagnostic services such as endoscopy, allergy detection, ultrasounds and much more to ensure that your pet is healthy all over.

Starting Price: $80

Surgical Services

We offer a large range of surgical services such as spay and neutering, orthopedic surgery and much more to ensure your pet can live a long and healthy life.

Starting Price: $150
